Project 1 — Interactive Audio & Visual with p5.js

Final: Sen Stu ↗

A simple interactive audio studio. Clicking on each image plays its corresponding sound. This piece uses images and audio to create an interactive experience rooted in everyday sensory cues.

Project 2 — HyperNarrative

Final: POLARIS ↗

POLARIS is a personal web narrative about finding your way through a dark moment. My freshman year, me and my friends were racially profiled and accused of a student conduct violation over a nerf gun — weapons charges, academic probation, a flag on our records. POLARIS walks through that journey in four chapters: Darkness, Distance, Light, and Return. Designed to feel like drifting through space, slowly finding your north star. Community got me through it. This project is my way of turning that into something people can actually feel.
